## Brain Dynamics Toolbox

### Technical Requirements
**Version 2026a** is the latest release. It works with **MATLAB R2020a** or newer, although we recommend **MATLAB R2023b** or newer for its improved DDE solver. Installation is as simple an unzipping the source code and adding its location to the matlab search path. See Chapter 1 of the Handbook for instructions.

### Handbook
The handbook is the offical guide to the Brain Dynamics Toolbox. It can be purchased from most online bookstores.
: **Heitmann S & Breakspear M (2026)** *Handbook for the Brain Dynamics Toolbox.* 9th Edition: Version 2026. bdtoolbox.org. Sydney, Australia. ISBN 978-0-6450669-4-4.

### Models
The toolbox ships with 60 example models. Users can publish their models on the Zenodo [Community Collection](https://zenodo.org/communities/bdtoolbox).
